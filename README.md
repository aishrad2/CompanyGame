# 2-Player Digital "Company" Game

A high-stakes game of luck and strategy for two players.

---

## **1. The Setup**

*   **The Board:** A grid of **16 mystery boxes**.
*   **Hidden Contents:**
    *   **11 Cash Boxes:** Contain varying amounts of cash.
    *   **3 Bomb Boxes:** Can end your turn or the entire game.
    *   **1 Exchange Box:** Swaps the scores between players.
    *   **1 Merchant Box:** Triggers a special "Risk vs. Reward" mini-game.

---

## **2. How to Play (The Flow)**

*   **Turn-Based Action:** Player 1 begins by picking a box.
*   **Cashing In:** If a cash box is revealed, the amount is added to the player's total. That player's turn continues, and they can keep picking boxes.
*   **Passing:** A player can choose to end their turn at any time (after revealing at least one cash box) to safeguard their current score, passing control to the other player.

---

## **3. Special Boxes**

### **The "Bomb" Rules**

The bombs are revealed sequentially and have different effects:
*   **Bomb #1:** The first player's turn ends immediately. Control passes to Player 2.
*   **Bomb #2:** The second player's turn ends immediately. Control passes back to Player 1.
*   **Bomb #3 (The Finale):** The player who reveals this box **loses the game instantly**. The other player wins the entire pot.

### **The Exchange Box**

*   If you open this, your total score and your opponent's total score are instantly swapped. This can be a game-changing move!

### **The "Digital Merchant" Box (Mini-Game)**

When this box is opened, the main game pauses for a special challenge:

1.  **The Item:** A luxury item is presented.
2.  **The Choice:** The player must choose one of two options:
    *   **Take the Cash:** Accept a guaranteed, safe cash amount and return to the main board.
    *   **The Appraisal:** Take a risk! Appraise the item for a chance to win a huge sum, but be prepared to walk away with very little.

---

## **4. How to Win**

There are two ways the game can end:

1.  **By Explosion:** A player clicks the **3rd Bomb**. They lose immediately, and the other player wins.
2.  **By Clear-Out:** All 16 boxes are opened without the 3rd bomb being triggered. The player with the **highest total cash** is declared the winner.

---

### **Summary of Controls**

| Action                 | Digital Result                                     |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Click Cash**         | Score increases. Player's turn continues.          |
| **Click Bomb 1 or 2**  | "TURN OVER" banner appears; control switches.      |
| **Click Bomb 3**       | "BOOM!" animation; Game Over screen appears.       |
| **Click Exchange**     | Scores animate and swap places between players.    |
| **Click Merchant**     | A pop-up mini-game screen opens.                   |
